FAQ

Before you enroll

Do the courses expire?

No. Every purchase — single track or all-access — is a one-time payment with lifetime access, including future updates to that content.

Is there a schedule I need to follow?

No cohorts, no fixed start dates. Modules unlock immediately and you move through them at your own pace.

What if a course isn't right for me?

7-day refund window from purchase, no questions asked, as long as you haven't completed more than 20% of the track.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes — a completion certificate is issued automatically once you finish all modules in a track.

Can I access courses on mobile?

Yes, the player works on any modern browser, desktop or mobile. No app required.
